üîÑ How it Works

  1. Click "Sign in with Google" - This sends you to /auth/google
  2. Redirect to Google - You'll be sent to Google's OAuth consent screen
  3. Authorize the app - Grant permission to access your email and profile
  4. Callback processing - Google redirects back to /auth/google/callback
  5. User info returned - You'll see a JSON response with your user information
